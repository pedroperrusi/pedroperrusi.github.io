About

Pedro Perrusi is a medical robotics research engineer at the University of Strasbourg. He is responsible for developing experiments for a novel robotic needle steering strategy developed in project Sperry.

His research is in the crossroads of physics-based simulation and medical robotics supported by the the ICube laboratory, INRIA MIMESIS team and the University Hospital of Strasbourg.
