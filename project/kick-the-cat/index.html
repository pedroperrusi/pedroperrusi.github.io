Hacking Health Camp 2019 - Kick the Cat

Mar 24, 2019
Project website (fr) Final Presentation (fr)
Indoor Santé Logo

Kick the Cat is a Serious game to promote physical activities using the 6 minutes walk test. A group of passionate volunteers met in Hacking Health Camp Strasbourg to work on the idea proposed by Indoor Santé. During the the weekend, we matured the idea and built a working prototype using Unity 3D, augmented reality and GPS tracking.
